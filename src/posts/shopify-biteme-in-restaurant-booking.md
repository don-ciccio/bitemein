BiteMe In provides essential tools for modern restaurant reservation management:

- **Real-time availability tracking** prevents double bookings and overbooking
- **Automated email confirmations** and reminder notifications reduce no-shows
- **Shopify theme integration** maintains consistent branding across your store
- **Centralized dashboard** for managing all reservations from your Shopify admin
- **Customizable time slots** and capacity controls for different service periods
- **Mobile-friendly booking** allows customers to reserve tables from any device

## Quick Setup Process

BiteMe In works for all restaurant types - from cafes and casual dining to fine dining establishments. The setup process is straightforward:

1. **Install** from the Shopify App Store
2. **Configure** your table capacity, time slots, and booking rules
3. **Customize** confirmation emails and notification settings
4. **Add** the booking widget to your store pages

No coding required - the app integrates seamlessly with your existing Shopify theme while maintaining your brand consistency.
